Definition of a GMO

What are Genetically Modified Organisms?

The definiton of a genetically modified organism, or GMO, changes depending on who you ask. Below I provide three definitons and their sources. The first definition is the one that I have constructed to be as unbaised as possible. The next two that follow come from baised views that extend from two institutions that have anticomplementary views on GMOs

1. A GMO is an organism that has had its genetic makeup modified in order to obtained a desired effect. The genetic makeup can be altered by removing genes, replacing genes, modifying existing genes, or adding new foreign genes.
